Privacy Policy 🌿

SoulGarden is designed to be your safe and peaceful space to reflect and grow emotionally. We respect your privacy deeply and are committed to keeping your data secure.

🔒 What We Store

  • Your Telegram ID and username (to identify your entries)
  • Your text memories, mood selections, and optional voice notes
  • Basic stats like points and streaks

👂 Voice Notes

Voice notes are stored on our server so you can listen to them in your personal garden. They are never shared publicly unless you choose to share your garden through "Explore".

🙈 Data Visibility

Memories shown in the Explore section are always anonymous. Your name is never displayed there.

🗑️ Deleting Your Data

You can delete all your data anytime using the /delete command in the bot. This action is permanent.

🔐 Encryption

While SoulGarden uses secure server storage, it is not end-to-end encrypted like messaging apps. Do not use the bot for sensitive or private information you wouldn’t want stored.

📩 Contact

If you have any concerns, feel free to reach out to the team via our Twitter.
