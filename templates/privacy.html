Privacy Policy 🌿

SoulGarden is your personal space for growth, peace, and self-reflection. Your privacy and trust are our priority.

🔒 What We Store

  • Your Telegram ID and username (to identify and personalize your experience)
  • Your text reflections, mood entries, and voice notes
  • Your stats: points, streaks, and engagement progress

🎧 Voice Notes

Your voice reflections are saved on our server to revisit in your private garden. We do not share them publicly unless you enable "Explore".

🕵️ Anonymous Mode

Anything shown publicly is always anonymous. Even in Explore, no usernames or IDs are revealed.

🗑️ Delete Your Data

Use the /delete command any time to permanently remove your data. This action is instant and final.

🔐 Is It Encrypted?

We use secure server storage, but it’s not end-to-end encrypted. Please don’t upload anything highly confidential.

💬 Questions?

Contact us via our Twitter or Telegram. We're here for you.
