🔒 SoulGarden Privacy Policy

Your privacy is sacred here. SoulGarden was created to help you reflect without judgment or surveillance.

🌿 What We Collect

  • Only the data you voluntarily share (memories, moods, voice notes).
  • Your Telegram user ID and username (for personalization).
  • Referral metadata for streaks and rewards.

🛡️ How It's Used

  • To show you your streaks, points, and memories.
  • To help you track emotional patterns and journaling growth.
  • To allow anonymous sharing via explore and visit garden features.

🧼 Deletion

You can delete all your data anytime using the 🗑️ Delete button. Everything will be wiped permanently, including voice notes and logs.

🔐 No Sharing

We never share your data with any third-party, ever.

💬 Questions?

If you have questions or concerns, Contact us via our Twitter.
